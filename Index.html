About Me


Shawn Jonas Wolf, was born 1978 in Honolulu, HI. He is the second born of five boys in his family (Power of two!). Shawn lives in Minneapolis, MN with his husband of 8 years, and beloved mate of 19 years in a nice little condo unit near downtown. Shawn has worked as an Mortgage Loan Underwriter, Operations and Credit Analyst for Wells Fargo Home Mortgage. Before that Shawn did a stent as a freelance fantasy artist.


Shawn has studied and traveled to China in 2014, though not fluent he has a good appreciation for the culture. With an Associates Degree in Software Development, Shawn now spends part of his time in a coding boot camp course at the U of M enhancing his coding abilities, as well as training his body at Los Campeones gym (https://loscampeonesgym.com/) under Tyler Caldwell (https://loscampeonesgym.com/trainer/tyler-caldwell/). Shawn's favorite color is Orange followed by Candy-Apple Red. Shawn's favorite foods are burgers, and pizza, favorite drinks include coffee, Knob Creek Manhattan's and orange juice. His favorite poison besides life is taking cruises with his husband.
